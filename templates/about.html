About This Dictionary Gbeɖeɖea Ŋuti

Purpose Taɖodzinu

This healthcare terminology dictionary was developed to bridge the communication gap between healthcare providers and Ewe-speaking patients. It provides translations of essential medical terms between English and Ewe, along with clear explanations and examples to ensure proper understanding of healthcare concepts.

Wowɔ lãmesẽ nyagbɔgblɔwo ƒe gbeɖeɖea sia be wòatsɔ akpe atsutsrɔe si le dɔyɔlawo kple dɔnɔ siwo ƒoa Eʋegbe la dome la. Egbeɖea lãmesẽ nyagbɔgblɔwo tso Yevugbe me yi Eʋegbe me, eye wòna ŋuɖoɖo kple kpɔɖeŋu siwo ana be amewo nase lãmesẽ ŋuti nyawo gɔme nyuie.

Features Emetɔ Vevitɔwo

  • Bilingual term translation (English-Ewe) Nyagbɔgblɔwo gbeɖeɖe le gbefɔwo dome (Yevugbe-Eʋegbe)
  • Detailed explanations with examples Ŋuɖoɖo tsitotsito kple kpɔɖeŋuwo
  • Categorization by medical field Nyagbɔgblɔwo ƒe mama ɖe lãmesẽ ƒe akpa vovovowo nu
  • Search and filtering capabilities Nyagbɔgblɔwo didi kple wo tiatia
  • Offline access to all content Àte ŋu azã esiawo katã ne interneɖo mele asiwò o
  • Feedback mechanism for continual improvement Mɔnu si dzi nàto aɖe wò susu agblɔ be woawɔ ɖɔɖɔɖo

How to Use Alesi Nàzãe
